About

TotalBiscuit was a British gaming commentator and critic on Youtube. He was best known for his "WTF Is" series on his youtube channel. He was also well-known for his roles as a professional shoutcaster and esports commentator. In 2014, he was diagnosed with cancer. However, he continued his work as games' critic nonetheless. In May 2018, his wife announced via Twitter that he had fallen into a coma and passed away.
